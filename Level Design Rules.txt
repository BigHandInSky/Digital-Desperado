digdes level design rules:

1 - The player must see the end tower in their centre of vision when starting a level
2 - The path to the end tower must be discernibile from the start (e.g. have towers dotting obstructed corners)
3 - Levels must be at least 10 seconds long
4 - The first Target must show what level of difficulty the level is:
	Easy 		- in plain view
	Medium 	- Obstructed but at least half is visible
	Hard 		- Mostly obstructed
	Puzzle		- Hidden from player starting area
5 - Levels must have a X by X wide starting area around the player
